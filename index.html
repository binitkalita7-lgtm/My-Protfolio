About the Studio

Kaushik Kakati — Design Visionary

With a passion for transforming raw spaces into curated environments, Kaushik Kakati founded Shaan Design with a singular vision — to bring world-class design sensibilities to Assam's growing residential and commercial landscape.

Every project is approached as a unique narrative, blending functionality with aesthetic excellence. From intimate bedrooms to grand commercial lobbies, we craft spaces that breathe, inspire, and endure.

What We Do

Our Expertise

01
Interior Design

Complete interior transformations for residential and commercial spaces. From concept development and space planning to material selection, furniture curation, and final execution — we handle every layer of your interior story.

02
Exterior Design

Facades that command attention. We design exteriors that are architecturally coherent, contextually appropriate, and visually arresting — from elevation finishes and cladding to entry sequences and boundary treatments.

03
Landscaping

Thoughtfully designed outdoor environments that extend your living and working spaces. Gardens, terraces, courtyards, and green corridors conceived as an integrated part of the overall design vision.

How We Work

Our Process

01
Discovery

We begin with a deep conversation — understanding your vision, lifestyle, budget, and timeline.

02
Concept Design

Mood boards, spatial layouts, and 3D visualizations bring the concept to life before a single nail is hammered.

03
Development

Detailed drawings, material schedules, and vendor coordination ensure flawless execution on site.

04
Handover

A meticulous final walk-through ensures every detail meets the design intent before you move in.
